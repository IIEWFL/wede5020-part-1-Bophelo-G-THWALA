About Us

We are a collective, collaborative, networking company

Our Mission

Our objective is to develop connections and relationships amongst civil tech groups so they can exchange expertise and enhance the communities they serve. Each of the organizations we support has access to resources, projects, and experience from people carrying out comparable work in other parts of the world thanks to the assistance of our global network. It is our job to come together with them, promoting resource sharing, and help them become more effective in their work. Our view is that digital technology may successfully promote social change through participation by the public, collaborative decision-making, and citizen-government relations.

How we work

We are networking and empowering the civic tech field and community. We always try new things, we benefit from activities such as:

  • Peer Learning

  • Redeploying Tools

  • Co-Creation

  • Capacity Building

Our Values

Greater positive impact can be achieved through teamwork. We work towards open, democratic societies and make decisions in a transparent way as a open-source organization.

  • Democracy

  • Collaboration

  • Openness

  • Accessibilty
